PROBLEM STATEMENT:

Managing daily tasks effectively is a challenge for many individuals, especially students and working professionals. People often forget tasks, miss deadlines, or struggle to organize their work efficiently due to lack of a structured system. Traditional methods like paper notes or mobile reminders are either inconvenient or too limited.
There is a need for a simple and efficient digital solution that allows users to record, track, and manage tasks in an organized manner.

SCOPE OF THE PROJECT:

The scope of the project includes:

1.Task creation
2.Task listing
3.Task updating
4.Task deletion

TARGET USERS:

1.Students who need to track academic tasks
2.Working professionals managing daily routines
3.Anyone who wants a simple digital task organizer
4.Users looking for a lightweight productivity tool without complex features

HIGH LEVEL FEATURES:

1.Add new tasks with descriptions
2.Display all tasks in a clear list format
3.Mark tasks as completed or pending
4.Delete tasks as needed
5.Structured, menu-driven workflow
6.Optional persistent storage using text/JSON files
7.Error handling for invalid inputs
